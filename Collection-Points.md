## Location and date

For the place of the collection point, we recommend schools/universities with engineering campuses. Public libraries (with 3D printers) and Fab labs are also great.

Next up is the date. Based on the filament waste data for a frequent user, the best dates would likely be once or twice a year.

## Advertisement

The advertisement is needed to let people know when, what and where you will be collecting. It is important that people know of the event in advance. In this repo you'll find some example advertisements that can be used for [displays]() TODO and [flyers]() TODO.


## Short ruleset of what IS an ISN'T being collected

If you partner up with a recycling facility, you should always check their filament sorting guidelines about what they recycle and what they do not recycle

## Labeled collection boxes

It is important that the waste is sorted per material type. Color and brand are usually ignored.

## Extra sorting system (OPTIONAL)

The use of such a sorting system is to be able to detect errors in the sorting done by people. This will improve the quality of the batch that can be sent to the recycling facilities.

We have not yet tested our sorting system, but it is based on the principle of sorting the material by density. For PLA and PETG separation, you should use a liquid which has a density in between those materials. When you put the materials in this liquid, some will sink and some will float. The perfect liquid for differentiating between PLA and PETG is glycerol (hypothetical).

We recommend that you test each sorted filament type seperately in such baths. If it was sorted correctly, all material should be floating/sinking/neutrally buoyant. If you see a clear difference in material behaviour, then the batch has some incorrect material types and these can now be easily filtered out.

## Contact with a nearby filament recycling facility OR your own filament maker

Obviously, you'll need a way to recycle the waste after collecting. You can contact a local 3D printing waste recycling facility or you can make your own if you have the right machine.

If you have neither of those, you could make something out of this waste to give away. Something that raises awareness among the people. In this repo we have provided you with some [keychain molds]() TODO which can be used to melt the plastic waste in.
